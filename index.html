Grateful Dead
I've always liked improvisational music, rock, and blues, and and to me the Dead combine those in a really engaging way. I don't usually say I'm a "Deadhead" because I sort of resist labels like that, but I do seem to listen to an awful lot of Grateful Dead and Dead and Company music. I've definitely seen GD/D&C more in concert than any other band.
  • Eyes of the World
  • Goin' Down the Road Feeling Bad
Blues
In my experience, most Deadheads (there's that word again!) like the Blues. I can't play them well, but I do enjoy listening.
  • Dust My Broom
  • Key to the Highway
1970s Rock and Roll
Some say I'm stuck in the past, and that may be true. But I have found that a lot of people I meet who play music, young and old, prefer to play songs from the 1960s and 1970s.
  • Stay With Me
  • Rock and Roll, Hootchie Koo
