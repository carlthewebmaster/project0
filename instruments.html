Instruments

In addition to my music gear, I also have a lot of instruments. Here's a list of some of my favorites:

Drum Kit
I have a Roland TD-3 vDrum kit that I bought as a very used floor model from Guitar Center. It has held up well.
Keyboard

I really like my Roland RD-300x keyboard. It sounds great and has weighted (piano-like) keys.

If I were to get another keyboard, it'd probably be a Nord Electro 6, which has fantastic sounds and un-weighted (organ-like) keys.

Bass Guitar
I bought my Yamaha bass from a friend for $70. Sounds ok but I always thought it wasn't very good. Then I saw a pretty good jazz guy in a hotel bar playing the same one, so I guess I got a good deal.
Guitars

I have a lot of guitars. Here are a few of them.

Fender Telecaster '52 Reissue
I love the sound of this guitar. It's all black with gold trim, and I got it by chance from a Guitar store in Colorado. I had played one and liked it, and the first one that came up when I went looking online was the one I bought.
Baby Taylor BT1
Small, light, and sounds fantastic. Not only that, it's great for travel!
Pignose Travel Electric with built-in amp.
It's very orange but the amp overdrives very nicely. I wish it would stay in tune better.
